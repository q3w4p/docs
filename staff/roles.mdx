## Hierarchy Overview

edates has a structured staff hierarchy. Each tier has specific permissions and limits. **Do not take actions outside your role's permissions.**

---

### Manager+

Full server control. Final say on all decisions.

- Handles escalated cases
- Manages the staff team --- hiring, firing, promotions
- Issues bans when lower staff lack perms
- Manages server integrity
- Ensure everything is in order
- Full server permission.
- Superior to anyone below.

---

### Head Of Staff

Final say on Staff.

- Handles escalated cases
- Manages the staff team --- hiring, firing, promotions
- Issues bans when lower staff lack perms
- Superior to anyone below.

---

### Admin

Manages staff below.

- Handles escalated cases
- Issues bans when lower staff lack perms
- Mentor any staff below
- Encourage other Staff members.

---

### Senior Moderator

Experienced moderators who assist Admins and handle complex cases.

- Can review and end jail cases (with cause)
- Handles appeals alongside Admins
- Mentors and monitors Trial Mods
- Has full moderation permissions including permanent bans

---

### Moderator

Core team --- handles stuff day to day.

- Full access to warns, mutes, timeouts, jails.
- Escalates ban-required situations if no ban perms
- Monitors all channels and voice

---

### Trial Moderator

Probationary period. Learning the ropes under supervision.

- Can warn and mute only
- **No bans, no ending jails prematurely**

<Warning>
  **Jails may not be ended prematurely unless the jailed member is proven innocent.** Only Admin+ may interfere with another staff member's jail case.
</Warning>

---

## Promotion Criteria

| Role               | Requirements                           |
| ------------------ | -------------------------------------- |
| Trial → Moderator  | Activity + Dedication.                 |
| Moderator → Senior | Demonstrated judgment + Admin approval |
| Senior → Admin     | Internal appointment only              |

---

<Note>
  Abusing your staff role at any rank is grounds for immediate demotion or removal.
</Note>

<Danger>
  This may change without prior notice.
</Danger>
