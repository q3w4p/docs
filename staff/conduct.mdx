## What's Expected of You

Being staff means being held to a higher standard than regular members — both in how you moderate and how you conduct yourself day-to-day.

---

## Core Standards

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Stay Professional" icon="briefcase">
    Remain calm when dealing with difficult members. Do not argue publicly in chat. If a situation gets heated, take it to the staff channel. Example: a member is repeatedly baiting you in #general — don't engage publicly, move it to staff chat and issue a punishment through the bot, not through argument.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Be Impartial" icon="badge-check">
    Enforce rules equally — including on your friends. Favoritism or selective enforcement is a punishable offense. If a close friend of yours is posting inappropriate content, you still warn them. If you have a personal conflict with a member, **hand the case off** to another staff member immediately.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Always Give a Reason" icon="message">
    Every bot action (warn, mute, jail, ban) must include a clear, specific reason. Logs with no reason are useless. **"rule break" is not a reason.** Write what actually happened — e.g. `,warn @user Repeated spam in #general after verbal warning`.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Keep Staff Business Confidential" icon="lock">
    Do not share staff discussions, decisions, this guide, or the staff channel with regular members. This includes screenshotting staff chat and sending it to friends in the server.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Stay Active" icon="clock">
    If you'll be away for an extended period, notify an Admin in the staff channel. Unexplained inactivity for **3\+ days** may result in demotion. A quick heads-up is all it takes — we understand life happens.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Check History Before Acting" icon="history">
    Always run `,history @user` before issuing a punishment. A member on their 4th offense for the same thing deserves a harsher response than a first-timer. Never skip this step.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

---

## Things You Must Never Do

<Warning>
  Any of the following will result in **immediate removal** from the staff team.
</Warning>

- Punish members for personal reasons (revenge, dislike, past beef)
- Use your role to win arguments or threaten members with punishment
- Share member personal information with anyone
- Override or prematurely end another staff member's jail case (unless you are Admin\+)
- Take no action hoping another staff member will handle it
- Moderate while emotionally compromised
- Break any staff rules or regular server rules
