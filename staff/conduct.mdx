## What's Expected of You

Being staff means being held to a higher standard than regular members — both in how you moderate and how you conduct yourself in the server day-to-day.

---

## Core Standards

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Stay Professional" icon="briefcase">
    Remain calm when dealing with difficult members. Do not argue publicly in chat. Take it to the staff channel if needed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Be Impartial" icon="scale">
    Enforce rules equally — including on your friends. Favoritism or selective enforcement is a punishable offense. If you have a personal conflict with a member, **hand the case off** to another staff member.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Always Give a Reason" icon="message">
    Every bot action (warn, mute, jail, ban) must include a clear reason. Logs with no reason are useless. "rule break" is not a reason — be specific.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Keep Staff Business Confidential" icon="lock">
    Do not share staff discussions, decisions, this guide, or the staff channel with regular members.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Stay Active" icon="clock">
    If you'll be away for an extended period, notify an Admin in the staff channel. Unexplained inactivity for 14+ days may result in demotion.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Check History Before Acting" icon="history">
    Always run `,history @user` before issuing a punishment. A member on their 4th offense deserves a harsher response than a first-timer.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

---

## Things You Must Never Do

<Warning>
  Any of these will result in **immediate removal** from the staff team.
</Warning>

- Punish members for personal reasons (revenge, disliking them, etc.)
- Use your role to win arguments or threaten members
- Share member personal information with anyone
- Override or prematurely end another staff member's jail case (unless you are Admin+)
- Take no action hoping another staff member will handle it
- Moderate while emotionally compromised

---

## Incident Reporting Format

When logging a complex or escalated situation in the staff channel, use this format:

```
User: @username (ID: 000000000000000000)
Offense: [describe what happened]
Action Taken: [warn / mute / jail / ban / escalated]
Evidence: [screenshot link or description]
Notes: [anything relevant — alt accounts, context, etc.]
```
