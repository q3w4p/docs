## When to Escalate

Some situations are beyond your rank's permissions or require senior judgment. **Always escalate** when:

- You don't have ban perms but a ban is clearly needed — jail the member and ping someone with ban perms
- The offense involves **doxxing, sexual content leaks, or false allegations** — jail and ping Admin+
- You suspect a **coordinated raid** — ping Head Admin immediately
- A situation involves **a minor** in an inappropriate context
- You're unsure if a permanent ban is warranted
- The offending member is **another staff member**
- You have a **personal conflict of interest** with the member involved

---

## How to Escalate

1. Take immediate protective action if needed (jail, mute, lock channel)
2. Post in the **staff channel** with the incident report format:

```
User: @username (ID: 000000000000000000)
Offense: [describe what happened]
Action Taken: [what you've done so far]
Evidence: [screenshot or description]
Needs: [ban / admin review / case handling]
```

3. Ping the appropriate rank — `@Senior Moderator` or `@Admin`
4. Do **not** discuss the case publicly or with the member until a Senior Mod or Admin has reviewed it

---

## Rank-Specific Escalation

| Situation | Escalate To |
|---|---|
| Need a ban issued | Any staff with ban perms |
| Complex jail case | Senior Moderator |
| Doxxing, sexual leaks, false allegations | Admin+ |
| Raid in progress | Head Admin (immediately) |
| Another staff member breaking rules | Head Admin |

---

<Warning>
  Do not sit on an escalation. If it needs to go up the chain, do it right away — waiting makes situations harder to resolve.
</Warning>
