<Icon icon="triangle-alert" size={20} /> **Warns**

<Warning>
  These offenses follow a **warn-first** progression before mutes kick in. Never jump straight to a mute without a prior warn on record unless stated otherwise.
</Warning>

| Offense | 1st Offense | Escalation | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Spam** | `,warn` | \+5min mute per repeat | — |
| **Soft NSFW** | `,warn` | 10min → 30min → \+30min per offense | — |
| **Nazi / Hitler jokes or images** | `,warn` | 10min mute → \+10min per offense | — |
| **Extreme racism** | `,warn` | 10min mute → \+10min per offense | — |
| **DM leak threats** | Verbal warn | → `,warn` → 5min mute → \+5min per offense | — |
| **Extreme sexual convos** | Verbal warn | → `,warn` → 10min mute → \+10min–1hr per offense | Lenient if no one is uncomfortable and it isn't going too far — use judgment |
| **Extreme harassment** | Verbal warn | → `,warn` → 10min mute → \+10min–1hr per offense | Severity of situation determines increment |
| **Misuse of channels** | Verbal warn | → `,warn` → 5min mute | Posting in the wrong channel repeatedly after being told |
| **Minimodding** | Verbal warn | → `,warn` | Telling members what to do as if you're staff when you're not — let staff handle it |

<Tip>
  **Beef between members:** Don't warn unless it gets out of hand. De-escalate first. If it continues, 10min mutes to both parties — or just the one who keeps pushing it.
</Tip>
