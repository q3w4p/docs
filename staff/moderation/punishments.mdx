## ⚠️ Warns

These offenses follow a warn-first progression before mutes kick in.

| Offense | 1st Offense | Escalation |
|---|---|---|
| **Spam** | `,warn` | +5min mute per repeat offense |
| **Soft NSFW** | `,warn` | 10min mute → 30min mute → +30min per offense |
| **Nazi / Hitler jokes or images** | `,warn` | 10min mute → +10min per offense |
| **Extreme racism** | `,warn` | 10min mute → +10min per offense |
| **DM leak threats** | Verbal warn | → `,warn` → 5min mute → +5min per offense |
| **Comgroup jokes (764, etc.)** | Verbal warn | → `,warn` → 5min mute → +5min per offense |
| **Extreme sexual convos** | Verbal warn | → `,warn` → 10min mute → +10min–1hr per offense |
| **Extreme harassment** | Verbal warn | → `,warn` → 10min mute → +10min–1hr depending on severity |

<Tip>
  **Beef:** Don't warn for beef unless it gets out of hand. Try to de-escalate first. If it continues, issue 10min mutes to both parties (or just the person who keeps pushing it).
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Sexual convos:** You may be lenient on conversations that aren't making others uncomfortable and aren't going too far. Use your judgment.
</Tip>
